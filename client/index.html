About GDSC😎

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

Concept Of GDSC

Google collaborates with university students who are passionate about growing developer communities and supports them with starting student clubs on their campuses.

Target Audience

Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.

Why GDSC

By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
